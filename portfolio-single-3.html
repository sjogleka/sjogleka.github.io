Illegal Activity Recognition

Project Details

  • Domain : Computer Vision
  • Completed On : Jan 2016 - May 2016

Project Link

• Developed an algorithm to track vehicles and calculate their velocity with hypothesis as most vehicles on the street move with similar velocity and model can be developed to identify the vehicles deviating from average speed.
• During this project, I had the opportunity to learn some highly essential algorithms such as SVM (Support Vector Machine), HOG (Histogram of Oriented Gradients), GMM (Gaussian Mixture Model) and Kalman Filters.
• Successfully identified the illegal events on street with an accuracy rate of 66%.
